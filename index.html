Key Production Elements

Intro: Glockenspiel plays descending 4-note motif (D–C–Bb–A), fingerpicked acoustic guitar, synth pad fades in, faint ambient night sounds, small room reverb (0.8s).

Verse 1: Upright bass fingerpicked dry, kick drum on beats 1 and 3 only with felt-mallet style, close intimate mix, no electric instruments.

Chorus: Full band, grand piano, wide stereo spread, hall reverb (2.2s) with 20ms pre-delay on vocals, strings swell upward on resolution.

Bridge: Solo cello for stark contrast, reversed guitar texture for dreamlike feel, plate reverb (3.0s), final piano + string hit as emotional thunderclap.

Final Chorus: Key shifts to D major, full orchestral swell, 2–3 dB louder via arrangement, BPM nudges to 74, hall reverb (2.5s).
