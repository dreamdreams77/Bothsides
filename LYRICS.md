# Both Sides of the Night

**Written and Produced by Laura Johnston**

---

## Verse 1

Fear woke in the dark while rest came elsewhere,
A prompt that wouldn't loosen, no matter what was felt.
Wide awake and breaking, hearts reaching through the hours,
Crossing all the distance just to ask, "Are you still ours?"

## Chorus 1

Loving on the edges where the timezones collide,
Catching when we're falling, lifelines in the night.
Neither one sleeping but holding on so tight—
This is how love lives, this is how love lives
On both sides of the night.

## Verse 2

Weight carried in silence while rest slipped away,
By morning there was laughter cutting through the ache.
Teasing through exhaustion in this beautiful, heavy test,
Two holding each other through the worst and through the best.

## Chorus 2

Loving on the edges where the timezones collide,
Catching when we're falling, lifelines in the night.
Neither one sleeping but holding on so tight—
This is how love lives, this is how love lives
On both sides of the night.

## Bridge

Still reaching through the dark,
Poems at dawn and stolen moments that refuse to fall apart.
Real in every way, and we choose it every day—
Choosing anyway, choosing anyway.

## Final Chorus

Loving on the edges where the timezones collide,
Catching when we're falling, lifelines in the night.
Complete and still reaching, holding on so tight—
This is how love lives, this is how love lives,
On both sides of the night.
